Anti-Phisher

Phishing is the act of attempting to Steal Sensitive and in some ways Critical info such as usernames, passwords and credit card details, usually acting as a trustworthy entity in an electronic communication. Communication appearing to be from popular social websites, auction sites, online payments process or IT administrator is commonly used to lure the unsuspecting users. Phishing emails may contain URLs to the websites that are infected with malware or to the websites which purports to be a Legitimate Social Media Platform. Phishing starts with an email or other Electronic communication Media. The message is made as if that message is coming from a Legitimate Sender or a Verified Sender


Cyber Security Professionals are now looking for trustworthy and steady detection techniques for phishing websites detection. The Majority of the people who are becoming a victim to attacks like these are the People Who Comes under the Literate-Senior Citizen Category Who use the Web for General Purposes.
